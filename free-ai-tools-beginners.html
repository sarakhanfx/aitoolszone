How to Start as a Complete Beginner — Your First Week Plan

The best beginner AI starter stack according to experts is: ChatGPT for general help, Perplexity for research, NotebookLM for notes, Claude for writing, Canva for visuals, and Grammarly for cleanup. That small stack covers almost everything a beginner needs.

Day 1

Sign up for ChatGPT and Claude — both free. Ask them the same question and compare the answers. Notice the difference in writing style.

Day 2

Sign up for Canva. Create your first social media post using a free template. Add your own text in under 10 minutes.

Day 3

Try Perplexity AI. Research any topic you are curious about and notice how it shows you sources with every answer.

Day 4–7

Install Grammarly in your browser. Use NotebookLM with one of your own documents. Try Gamma to build a quick presentation.

🎯 Final Verdict

You do not need to pay anything to start using powerful AI tools in 2026. The free plans from ChatGPT, Claude, Canva, Perplexity, and NotebookLM are genuinely useful for real work — not just demos.

The only mistake beginners make is trying too many tools at once. Pick two or three from this list, use them for one week, and you will immediately see the difference in your productivity. Start free. Upgrade only when you need more.
